Generating images using a generative adversarial network (GAN)

Back to the main page.

In this course we covered one type of generative models called denoising diffusion probabilistic models. There are other methods to train generative models. One example is generative adversarial networks (GANs).

Build a GAN to generate pictures of handwritten digits. Train your GAN on the MNIST dataset. You can start learning about GANs by reading the original paper.

Task steps:

  • 1. Describe the idea behind GANs and the model architecture you are going to use.
  • 2. Implement your model in PyTorch.
  • 3. Train your model and generate some samples.

You will be graded based on your code and understanding of the problem.
