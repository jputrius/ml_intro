Part-of-speech tagging

Back to the main page.

The goal of part-of-speech tagging is to categorize words in a sentence based on their part of speech (nouns, verbs, adjectives etc.). Read more about part-of-speech tagging on wikipedia

Build a transformer and LSTM based models to perform part-of-speech tagging.

Task steps:

  • 1. Describe the model architectures you are going to use.
  • 2. Implement your models in PyTorch.
  • 3. Compare your models' performance on this dataset. You can find the explanation of the tags here.

You will be graded based on your code and understanding of the problem.
